About The Curve Africa

Who We Are

The Curve Africa is a software engineering academy focused on developing technology talent across Africa. The program provides young Africans with the opportunity to learn practical tech skills through structured training, mentorship, and real-world projects.

Our Mission

At The Curve Africa,we are dedicated to empowering the youth ofAfrica through our comprehensive software engineering academy.With a firm belief in the potential of youngindividual, we provide mentorship and on-site trainingprograms forthose who are eager to learn and commit to a transformative 6-month journey.

Our Vision

The vision of The Curve Africa is to create a strong community of technology professionals across Africa and train thousands of young people in technology.

Our Program

The Curve Africa offers an intensive training program where students learn software engineering, collaborate on projects, and gain hands-on experience through mentorship and practical learning.
